Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services effectively:

  • Automatically Collected: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage data through analytics
  • Voluntarily Provided: Email address when contacting support
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for website functionality and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our website services
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Analyze website usage patterns
  • Ensure website security and prevent abuse

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage analysis
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • Ahrefs Analytics: For SEO and traffic analysis
  • Google Drive: For file hosting and downloads

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their use of your information.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do not store personal information beyond what's necessary for our services.

5. Downloads and File Access

When you download templates or resources from our website, we may log download statistics for analytical purposes. No personal information is required for downloads.

6. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Provide personalized advertisements

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

9. International Users

Our website is hosted and operated from Pakistan. If you access our site from other countries, your information may be transferred to and processed in Pakistan.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@engisimulation.com.
